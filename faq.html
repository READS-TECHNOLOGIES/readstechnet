Frequently Asked Questions

About $READS

What is $READS?

$READS is an educational cryptocurrency token that rewards students for learning and excelling in their studies.Its mission is to create a learning environment where teachers and learners can come together to learn and earn real value.

What is the vision of $READS?

The vision of $READS is to make quality education profitable, accessible, and transparent using the power of blockchain. It was created to solve the problem of students losing interest in their studies due to a lack of incentives and high exam failure rates.

How It Works

How does the "Learn-to-Earn" model function?

The Learn-to-Earn model allows users to earn $READS tokens by studying educational content and passing quizzes based on the material. Learners receive rewards for completing lessons, quizzes, and certifications, which creates a direct incentive to study.

What are the key features of the platform?

The platform is a mobile-first app powered by Flutter and integrated with blockchain.It includes a CBT engine with interactive quizzes, mock exams for tests like WAEC and JAMB, and provides automated grading to help learners identify weaknesses.Users also receive blockchain-based certificates upon course completion.

How can I access $READS and the platform?

The platform is accessible via a native app, which ensures a broad reach.To provide access to users without smartphones or in low-connectivity areas, tokens can also be accessed and transactions can be processed via USSD/IVR support.

About the Token

What can I use $READS tokens for?

$READS is a utility token that can be used for a variety of purposes with the ecosystem. These include paying teachers and tutors in-app, purchasing eBooks, paying for advanced courses, and even paying for exam registration fees with partner centers.

What is the total supply of the $READS token?

The total supply of the $READS token is 100 Billion on the Ethereum (ERC-20) blockchain.
