How Nigerian Students Can Earn Crypto While Preparing for JAMB 2026

Let's be real — JAMB prep is stressful. You're studying past questions, memorizing formulas, and praying your CBT score is high enough to secure your admission. And on top of all that, there's no reward beyond the result itself.

But what if every hour you spent studying could actually put money in your pocket? Not someday — right now, as you prepare. That's exactly what $READS is building.

What is $READS? It's a Learn-to-Earn platform where Nigerian students earn $READS tokens (on the Cardano blockchain) just by studying, completing quizzes, and passing practice exams — including JAMB mock tests.

Why JAMB Preparation Is the Perfect Match for Learn-to-Earn

JAMB candidates already do the work — they study, they practice, they test themselves repeatedly. The only thing missing is a reward system. Think about it: you're already spending weeks reviewing Biology, Government, Mathematics, and English. $READS simply makes that effort count twice — once for your score, and once for your wallet.

This isn't a scam or "do this survey and earn ₦500" kind of thing. The tokens you earn are real crypto assets on the Cardano blockchain, with genuine utility — you can use them to pay for exam registrations, access premium study content, and more as the ecosystem grows.

How It Actually Works — Step by Step

1. Sign Up and Set Your Exam Goals

When you join the $READS platform, you pick your subject areas and set your target exam — in this case, JAMB UTME. The platform personalises your study path based on your subject combination (e.g. Science: Maths, Physics, Chemistry, English).

2. Study Structured Lessons

You get curated lessons for each JAMB subject — broken into short, focused modules. These aren't random notes copy-pasted from the internet. They're structured to align with the JAMB syllabus so every minute counts for your actual exam.

3. Take Quizzes and CBT Practice Tests

After each lesson, you take a quiz. The CBT engine simulates the actual JAMB interface — timed questions, multiple choice format, instant grading. Pass a quiz, earn $READS tokens. It's that simple.

4. Tokens Go Straight to Your Wallet

Every completed module and passed quiz credits $READS tokens directly to your in-app wallet. No waiting, no middleman. The blockchain records it automatically via smart contract.

Tips to Maximise Your Earnings Before JAMB

  1. Be consistent — daily short sessions earn more over time than cramming once a week.
  2. Focus on your weak subjects first — the AI Analytics dashboard shows where you're losing marks. Fix those areas and you'll earn more from the improvement quizzes.
  3. Complete full modules — partial lessons don't trigger full rewards. Finish each module to unlock the quiz and the tokens.
  4. Upgrade to Premium — Premium users earn double token rewards on every activity.
  5. Join the community — the $READS Telegram group shares study tips, motivational check-ins, and early platform updates.
