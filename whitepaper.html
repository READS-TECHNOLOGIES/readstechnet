Section 1

The Problem

Nigeria has over 200 million people with one of the youngest populations in the world. Every year, more than 1.7 million candidates sit JAMB. Millions more prepare for WAEC, NECO, and BECE. Yet the system fails them in three fundamental ways:

1.1 Quality Education is Expensive

Private lesson teachers, quality textbooks, CBT practice centres, and exam registration fees represent significant financial burden for the average Nigerian family. Students in rural areas or low-income households are systematically disadvantaged before the exam begins.

1.2 CBT Practice is Inadequate

JAMB moved to Computer-Based Testing, but the majority of Nigerian students have never practised on a CBT interface before their exam day. Many underperform not because they lack knowledge — but because they lack practice with the format.

1.3 No Reward for Academic Effort

A student can spend 4–6 hours studying per day and receive nothing beyond a score on an exam. There is no mechanism to reward the effort itself — only the outcome. This creates low motivation and high dropout rates.

The Core GapStudents need resources to study. Studying takes time. Time has value. But no existing platform in Nigeria pays students for the time they invest in their education.
Section 2

Our Solution

$READS is a Learn-to-Earn platform that rewards students with $READS tokens for studying. Every lesson completed, every quiz passed, every study streak maintained — earns real crypto rewards usable within the platform ecosystem or held as a digital asset.

  • Education First: The product is a genuine study tool. The Learn-to-Earn mechanic is the reward layer on top of a real educational experience.
  • Accessible by Default: Students can earn tokens for free — no upfront crypto purchase required.
  • Transparent & Trustworthy: All token distributions governed by smart contracts. Fees held in escrow, released only when conditions are verified.
  • Built for Nigeria, Ready for Africa: Designed around Nigerian exams first, with a roadmap to expand across Africa in Phase 5.
Section 3

Platform Overview

3.1 Learn-to-Earn Module

Students access lessons organised by school class, subject, term, and week — aligned to the Nigerian curriculum. Each lesson has a minimum read time enforced. After reading, students complete a quiz. Passing above a threshold score triggers a token reward. Premium users earn 2× tokens. Anti-cheat mechanisms including rate limits, cooldowns, and minimum response times prevent gaming.

3.2 Exam Preparation (CBT Practice)

Full simulated CBT exams in JAMB, WAEC, NECO, BECE, SAT, and IELTS format. Timed, randomised, with instant score feedback. The platform also handles real exam registration — students pay in $READS, held in escrow until a verified CBT centre uploads proof of registration.

3.3 School Partner Dashboard

Schools, CBT centres, and tutors apply to become platform partners. On admin approval, a unique school code is generated and emailed to the institution. Staff roles — Principal, Academic Officer, Finance Officer — have scoped permissions. Partners pay zero onboarding fees. Every approved partner receives a 500 $READS welcome bonus. $READS earns only transaction fees from partner activity.

Partner Pricing ModelPartners set their prices in local fiat currency (₦, $, or £). The platform automatically converts to $READS at the real-time market rate at the moment of payment — so neither partners nor students are exposed to token price volatility.

3.4 AI Tutor

An integrated AI tutor powered by Groq's LLaMA 3.3 70B model allows students to ask questions on any subject and receive intelligent, curriculum-aware responses. Available to all users; unlimited for premium subscribers.

3.5 EduContent Marketplace

A peer-to-peer marketplace where students and educators sell study notes, past question packs, summaries, and other educational content. All transactions in $READS. Moderated by platform admins.

5.1 Token Burn Mechanism

$READS implements a deflationary burn mechanism. 20% of every transaction fee collected is permanently burned — removed from circulation forever. The remaining 80% flows to READS Technologies as platform revenue.

Burn SourceRate
Exam registration fees20% burned

Expired / unclaimed student rewards100% burned
Cancelled exam escrow residuals100% burned
What Burning Never TouchesStudent reward earnings, partner welcome bonuses, presale buyer allocations, and the team vesting pool are completely excluded from the burn mechanism. Burning only ever comes from platform revenue.

5.2 Price Stability Layer

Burning reduces supply and can push token price upward. Left unmanaged, higher prices could burden students paying for services in $READS. The solution: partners set all prices in fiat currency. The platform converts to $READS at real-time market rate at the moment of payment.

Supported pricing currencies: Nigerian Naira (₦) for schools, CBT centres and local tutors · US Dollar ($) for international partners · British Pound (£) for SAT and IELTS exam partners.

Worked ExampleA school sets its term fee at ₦50,000. If $READS = ₦10, the student pays 5,000 $READS. If $READS rises to ₦25, the student pays only 2,000 $READS. The partner always receives ₦50,000 worth of value. The student always pays fair market rate. Appreciation benefits holders — it never burdens spenders.
