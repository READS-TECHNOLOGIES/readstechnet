About the $READS Token

The $READS token is the backbone of our educational ecosystem. Students are empowered to earn tokens for their dedication to learning, successfully completing tests, and active participation in various educational activities on our platform.

These earned tokens hold significant utility within the $READS community. They can be seamlessly used to register for national and international examinations, unlock exclusive premium content, access advanced study materials, and much more, providing a tangible reward for academic growth.

Please note: USSD is currently used for transaction processing within our ecosystem, not for direct token earning.

Tokenomics & Distribution

The $READS token distribution is designed to ensure a fair and sustainable ecosystem, prioritizing community growth and platform development. Our allocation strategy supports long-term value and incentivizes active participation.

60% - Learn-to-Earn Rewards

Dedicated to incentivizing students for their academic achievements and engagement on the platform.

10% - Ecosystem Development

Funds for platform upgrades, new features, content creation, and infrastructure maintenance.

15% - Marketing & Partnerships

For community building, global outreach, and strategic collaborations to expand the $READS ecosystem.

10% - Team & Advisors

Allocated to retain and reward the core team and expert advisors (with vesting schedules).

5% - Liquidity & Reserves

Ensuring market stability and providing a buffer for unforeseen circumstances.
