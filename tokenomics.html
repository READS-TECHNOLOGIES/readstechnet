Partner Economics

Partner Token Model

Partners are stakeholders, not customers. Zero fees to join. Real token rewards for participating.

🎁
500 $READS Welcome Bonus
Every approved partner — school, CBT centre, or tutor — receives 500 $READS on approval. No conditions, no strings.
🚫
Zero Onboarding Fees
Partners pay nothing to join. They're already taking a risk by integrating a new token into their operations. We don't add to that.
💸
Transaction Fees Only
$READS earns a platform fee on exam registrations, school fees, and tutor sessions — deducted from escrow. Partners only contribute when they earn.
The Alignment LogicPartners holding their 500 $READS welcome bonus benefit directly from token price appreciation as the platform grows. A school pushing students to use $READS is also making its own held tokens more valuable. That's intentional.

Why This System Is Self-Reinforcing

Burning reduces supply → scarcity pushes price up → students need fewer tokens for the same service → token becomes more attractive to hold → demand rises → price rises further → more tokens burned per cycle. Every part of the loop strengthens every other part.
