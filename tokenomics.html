Partner Economics

Partner Token Model

Partners are stakeholders, not customers. Zero fees to join. Real token rewards for participating.

🎁
500 $READS Welcome Bonus
Every approved partner — school, CBT centre, or tutor — receives 500 $READS on approval. No conditions, no strings. Our first act of trust.
🚫
Zero Onboarding Fees
Partners pay nothing to join. No subscription, no listing fee, no setup charge. They're already taking a risk integrating a new token. We don't add to that.
💸
Transaction Fees Only
$READS earns a platform fee on exam registrations, school fees, and tutor sessions — deducted from escrow. Partners only contribute when they're earning.
The Alignment Logic Partners holding their 500 $READS welcome bonus benefit directly from token price appreciation as the platform grows. A school pushing its students to use $READS for CBT practice is also making its own held tokens more valuable. That's not an accident — it's the design.

Why This System Is Self-Reinforcing

Burning reduces supply → scarcity pushes price up → students need fewer tokens for the same real-world service → token becomes more attractive to hold → demand rises → price rises further → transaction values grow → more tokens burned per cycle. Every part of the loop strengthens every other part.
